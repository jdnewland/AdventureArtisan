Adventure Artisan

About

Adventure Artisan is a web tool designed to help Dungeon Masters quickly generate engaging D&D adventure prompts. Mix and match character, goal, location, obstacle, and twist cards to create unique adventure scenarios.

Examples of Using the Cards

  • Basic Adventure: Combine one character, goal, location, obstacle, and twist card for a straightforward prompt.
  • Dynamic Conflict: Choose two characters with conflicting goals and see how they interact at a specific location.
  • Complex Motivations: Select two characters and separate them by motivations, locations, or events.
  • Choice-Driven Plot: Create a scenario where a character must choose between two different goals while facing a significant obstacle.

Use these combinations to spark creativity and design unique adventures!
