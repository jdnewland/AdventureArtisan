About

Adventure Artisan is a web tool designed to help Dungeon Masters quickly generate engaging D&D adventure prompts. Mix and match character, goal, location, obstacle, and twist cards to create unique adventure scenarios.

Prompt Styles

  • Quest for Glory: A straightforward prompt with one of each card type.
  • Wheel of Destiny: A complex prompt with multiple goals, obstacles, and characters.
  • Battle of Minds: Conflicting goals from two characters leading to the same obstacle.
  • Fractured Soul: A character facing a choice between two goals with a significant obstacle.
